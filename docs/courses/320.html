BADM 320 - Principles of Marketing Core Course

Instructor: Liu
Semester: wi25
Core Requirement: Yes

Learning Objectives

  • understand the roles of marketing in companies and in society
  • learn the concepts, theories, and frameworks of modern marketing practice
  • obtain knowledge and experience needed to evaluate marketing environment
  • design and implement successful marketing programs with decisions on product, pricing, distribution, and promotion.
